AI Books and Videos

Books

Deep Learning For Coders With Fastai and PyTorch
Jeremy Howard, Sylvain Gugger (2020)
This book is aimed people with no AI experience and a background in programming. There is also a free version of the book on Github - FastAI Fastbook.

Grokking Machine Learning
Luis G. Serrano (2021)
A great introduction to machine learning and AI.

Hands-On Large Language Models
Jay Alammar, Maarten Grootendorst (2024)
This book covers all aspect of Transformers and LLMs.

Cracking the Coding Interview
Gayle McDowell (2015)
If you need to brush up on coding algorithms, big-o notation and best practices for coding interviews this is the book for you. Although not specifically AI related it's highly beneficial to be familiar with the coding concepts in this book.

The Deep Learning Book
Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio and Aaron Courville (2016)
A free online book which covers all aspects of Deep Learning fundamentals. This book is aimed at students who are interested in learning about AI.
