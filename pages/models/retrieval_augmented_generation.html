Retrieval Augmented Generataion

The term Retrieval Augmented Generataion (RAG) is a general term relating to AI architectures that leverage a custom knowledgebase to augment responses from LLMs. This allows the system to be very effective at providing accurate responses based on local business knowledge. The RAG knowledgebase can be loaded with all relevant business documents, emails, procedures, spreadsheets and more. RAG was first introducted in the following paper.

The simplest RAG systems comprise of 3 main components:

  1.  A Knowledgebase which stores data
  2.  A Retriever which searches the data
  3.  A Generator (LLM) which provides a response based on the retireved data
